Why Choose IEEE?

Join a community of over 420K+ technology and engineering professionals united by a common desire to continuously learn, interact, collaborate, and innovate. Get the resources and opportunities you need to keep on top of changes in technology.

Professional Networking

Chance to network with high-end professionals, scholars and like-minded people who are of high value.

Discounts

Reduced rates on various products and services including conferences, events, competitions and resources.

Resources

Access to thousands of scientific journals, ebooks, articles, events, activities, competitions which are of high value exclusively.

Updates

Up-to-date knowledge on technical events, fests, competitions taking place across the whole world, at your fingertips.

Opportunity

Innovative events and workshops that strengthen technical, interpersonal and networking skills for all parties involved.
