KTU Study Resources

Tip

For getting S grades in almost all subjects or even 10 SGPA, all you need to do is study the particular subject with Notes (PDFs or class notes) and/or YouTube videos and then refer or even manually solve previous question papers. This will guarantee a good grade in almost all cases. Ideally, download the syllabus and ensure you have studied all the individual topics per module.

Browsing the internet, searching for these resources is frustrating. This webpage has been built to solve this issue. About 90% of the resources you will ever need for studying in APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) is linked here.

In future, this webpage will also contain many general learning resources as well (like useful websites).

Most Popular Teachers / Content Sources / YouTube Channels

For KTU exams, searching by subject code (e.g., CST301 KTU) often gives the best playlist. Experienced KTU students recommend using the syllabus and searching module-by-module if a full playlist is not available.
